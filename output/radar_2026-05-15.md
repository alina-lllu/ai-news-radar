> 来源：The Verge | [链接](https://www.theverge.com/science/931766/arxiv-ai-slop-ban-researchers)

> 来源：The Verge | [链接](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/931544/openai-keeps-shuffling-its-executives-in-bid-to-win-ai-agent-battle)

备受关注的马斯克诉奥特曼案本周结束庭审。双方在结案陈词中反复围绕一个核心问题：我们能否信任掌控 AI 发展方向的领导者？此案的判决结果可能对 AI 行业的治理模式产生深远影响。
> 来源：TechCrunch | [链接](https://techcrunch.com/podcast/the-openai-trial-wraps-up-and-the-musk-founder-machine-keeps-spinning/)
