> 来源：TechCrunch AI | https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/08/databricks-matei-zaharia-wins-acm-computing-prize-agi/

> 来源：MIT Technology Review | https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/04/08/1135398/mustafa-suleyman-ai-future/

> 来源：TechCrunch AI | https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/07/google-quietly-releases-an-offline-first-ai-dictation-app-on-ios/

### 11. Atlassian launches visual AI tools and third-party agents in Confluence
Atlassian为其协作软件Confluence推出了视觉AI工具和第三方AI代理集成。用户现在可以在软件内直接创建视觉资产，并能与Lovable、Replit和Gamma等平台的AI代理协同工作。
> 来源：TechCrunch AI | https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/08/atlassian-confluence-visual-ai-tools-agents/

### 12. Poke makes AI agents as easy as sending a text

> 来源：TechCrunch AI | https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/08/poke-makes-ai-agents-as-easy-as-sending-a-text/
